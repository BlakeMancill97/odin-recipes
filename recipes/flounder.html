Baked Flounder with Parmesan Crumbs

By adding freshly grated parmesan to the breadcrumbs, you can create a tasty and buttery topping for this flaky white fish. Cooks in just fifteen minutes!

    Ingredients:

  • 4 flounder fillets (2lbs total)
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 3/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup coarse fresh bread crumbs
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

    Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425F
  2. In a large baking dish, season the fish fillets with salt and pepper
  3. Mix the the parmesan with the breadcrumbs, melted butter, and olive oil and sprinkle over the fillets.
  4. Bake for fifteen minutes, or until the fish is cooked and the topping is golden
  5. Let stand for five minutes before cooking
