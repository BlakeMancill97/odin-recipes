Taste of Fall

For those who wish for a stronger addition to their fall celebrations.

    Ingredients:

  • 1 oz Fireball cinnamon whiskey
  • 2 oz spiced apple cider
  • 1 oz lemon-lime soda
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 orange slice
  • 1 pinch powdered cloves

    Directions

  1. Add ice to a rocks glass
  2. Add the whiskey then the cider and the soda, no need to mix
  3. Add the cinnamon stick followed by the orange slice
  4. Dust the top of the orange with the powdered cloves
